  5 save as TypeCzech.no-comments.js

  6  npx google-closure-compiler --language_out ECMASCRIPT_2020 --js=TypeCzech.no-debug.js --js_output_file=TypeCzech.min.js

  7 copy TypeCzech.min.js to /web-resources/
